Slayt Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Slayt processes account information, uploaded media, and connected platform metadata in order to provide content planning, scheduling, and publishing features.

Information We Process

This may include your account email, profile information, uploaded media files, connected social account identifiers, and publishing metadata such as titles, descriptions, schedules, and privacy settings.

How Information Is Used

Slayt uses this information to authenticate users, store planned content, connect to third-party publishing APIs, and carry out publishing actions you explicitly request.

Third-Party Services

When you connect platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram, Slayt exchanges and stores tokens needed to access those services on your behalf. Those platforms have their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Retention

Data is retained as needed to operate the service and maintain your workspace unless it is deleted by you or removed as part of service administration.

Contact
